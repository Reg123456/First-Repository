The JOE's Meet & Celebrate at The Ritz-Carlton, Bangalore presents an inspiring selection of beverages, masterful appetizers and confections in a relaxed, contemporary environment.

Choose between inventive cocktails, fresh fruit juices, eclectic smoothies and dedicated menus for coffees and loose teas. Hand-crafted pastries and sweet treats contrast with flavorful savory appetizers, both of which contribute to the unforgettable Ritz-Carlton Afternoon Tea, served every day of the week. An impressive library and complimentary wireless internet service capture the effortless comfort of a well-appointed living room, providing guests with a cozy retreat in the midst of busy Bangalore.

  • The Legendary JOE's Meet & Celebrate Afternoon Tea is served everyday
  • Coffee and loose tea menus
  • Freshly-baked pastries and confections
  • Fresh fruit juices and smoothies
  • Live entertainment on weekend evenings
  • Extensive library and complimentary wireless internet access
